Installation:


   There has been some problems in the past with the understanding of the installation, as the instructions weren't very clear. They did not take into consideration that not all users were used to installing Zen-Cart modules. So, in this version, I'm going to try to clear that up a bit.

   Once you have downloaded the zip file to your pc, unzip it. Look in the folder tree and you will find a folder called "CUSTOM" in "includes/templates/CUSTOM". You will need to rename that folder to whatever your custom templates' name is. Let's say your custom template's name is "orange". Then the folder structer after you rename the CUSTOM folder should look like this: "includes/templates/orange/".

   After you have that done, then using your FTP client, upload the entire "includes" folder (leaving all other folders in tact) to the root of your Zen-Cart site. By leaving the folder structure in tact on your computer, your FTP client will place all included files in the proper place on your site.

Turning the Menu "on":


   After the above step is complete, log into your Admin area on your site and navigate to Admin>Tools>Layout Boxes Controller. There you will find a new box (and have a warning at the top of the page that a new box has been found)called categories_css.php. Turn that on, placing it on which ever side and what ever order you desire.

 

Styling Your Menu:


   In order to style your menu to your deired needs, such as colors, font sizes, margins, paddings, and such, you will need to have a little knowledge of css. You can find the stylesheet for this menu in: "includes/templates/CUSTOM/css>stylesheet_categories_menu.css"
If you just want to change the arrows, then look in "includes/templates/CUSTOM/images/arrows", find the ones you would like to use and make note of their number.
Then, in the stylesheet, you'll see two sections of code that call these images:

This section is for the hover-off arrow:

#nav-cat li.submenu {
   background: url(../images/arrows/arrow40.png) 99% 50% no-repeat;
   background-color: #6495ed;
}

This section is for the hover-on arrow:

#nav-cat li.submenu:hover {
   background: url(../images/arrows/arrow62.png) 99% 50% no-repeat;
}

All you have to do here, is change the number(s) from 40 and 62 to the number(s) of the arrows that you would like to use.
